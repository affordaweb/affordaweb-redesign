Pricing FAQ

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

No setup fees and no hidden costs. You pay one flat monthly rate and everything listed in your plan is included. The price you see is the price you pay.

What happens if I need more than 10 pages on the Standard plan?

If your project grows beyond what Standard covers, you can upgrade to the Premium plan. Contact us and we will take care of the transition.

Can I cancel my plan at any time?

Yes. All our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel at any time.

Is the domain name included in the price?

Domain registration is not included in the monthly plan price, but we can help you register your domain and connect it to your new website.

How do unlimited updates work on Standard and Premium plans?

Send us your changes via the contact form or by email, and we will implement them within 24 hours. Text, photos, new pages — there is no limit to how many requests you can submit each month.

Can I upgrade from Basic to Standard or Premium later?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time. We will build out the additional pages and features on your new plan without any additional setup fees.

Do you offer e-commerce websites?

Yes. The Premium plan includes e-commerce functionality for up to 30 products. Reach out to discuss your store requirements before choosing a plan.

How do I get started?

Reach out via hello@affordawebsolutions.com or fill out the contact form. We will follow up to learn about your goals and recommend the right plan.

Not Sure Which Plan Is Right for You?

Contact us for a free consultation. We will recommend the right plan based on your goals and budget.
