Pricing FAQ

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

No setup fees and no hidden costs. You pay one flat monthly rate and everything listed in your plan is included. The price you see is the price you pay.

What happens if I need more than 10 pages on the Standard plan?

If your project requires more pages than your current plan allows, you can easily upgrade to the Premium plan. Contact us and we will handle the migration seamlessly.

Can I cancel my plan at any time?

Yes. All our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel at any time with 30 days notice. We will export your content so you are never left stranded.

Is the domain name included in the price?

Domain registration is not included in the monthly plan price, but we can help you register your domain and connect it to your new website. Domain registration typically costs $10–$15/year.

How do unlimited updates work on Standard and Premium plans?

Simply email us your requested changes — new text, photos, pages, or any other updates — and we will implement them within 24–48 business hours. There is no limit to how many update requests you can submit per month.

Can I upgrade from Basic to Standard or Premium later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade your plan at any time. We will build out the additional pages and features on your upgraded plan without any additional setup fees.

Not Sure Which Plan Is Right for You?

Contact us for a free consultation. We will recommend the best plan based on your goals, timeline, and budget.
